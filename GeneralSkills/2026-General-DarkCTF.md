## 🕵️ Phase 1: Reconnaissance & Analysis
* **Initial Findings:** 
The Chase Begins
Like any self-respecting CTF player, I immediately assumed there was more to it. I checked their Twitter. Scrolled through Instagram. Visited their website. Nothing obvious jumped out. I was already mentally preparing to fire up Burp Suite on their socials (please don't do this) when someone mentioned checking the Discord.
So I hopped into the CrackOn Community Discord and navigated to the #announcements channel.
There it was - the big welcome message for Dark CTF. Rules, regulations, flag format, the usual boilerplate. I skimmed it quickly. Nothing. I was about to leave when something at the very bottom caught my eye.
A spoiler tag.
You know those ||like this|| blocks on Discord - the ones that hide text until you click them? There was one sitting quietly at the end of the announcement, completely easy to scroll past.
I clicked it.
