## 📝 How to Log a New Intel Report

1.  Create a folder within the relevant sub-category (e.g., `Geospatial/`).
2.  Use the `OSINT_TEMPLATE.md` to document your steps, including:
    * **The Initial Lead:** The image or data point provided.
    * **The Pivot:** How you moved from Point A to Point B.
    * **The Confirmation:** The final coordinate or flag.
3.  **Update this file** by adding your new report to the "Operation Index" table above.
